- 7 / 1 / 92 -

his is one of the most famous editions of Off The Hook ever to air. And even though it was scheduled twice, it has never aired in its entirety. The program deals with the ease of listening in on cellular phone calls by demonstrating actual conversations that were not only overheard, but actually interfered with by an anonymous hacker. This program was prerecorded and for some reason the last few minutes never made it onto the air. When the show was rescheduled later in the summer, it was yanked midway by station management who thought it was too controversial even for WBAI.
