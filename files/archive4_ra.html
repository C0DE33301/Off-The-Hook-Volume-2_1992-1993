You can listen to episodes of "Off The Hook" by clicking on the "play" button on the RealAudio console on each page. The realaudio files have been embedded so you must wait for them to be downloaded prior to playing them.

You will need the most current version of the Real Audio player to hear them. The software must be installed and configured as a plug-in for Netscape Navigator and/or Microsoft's Internet Explorer.

Synopsis: October - December 1995, 1996 - 1997



As "Off The Hook" moved to a new timeslot, the dramatic stories brought to the listeners of New York showed no signs of abating. This was when the shocking events of the Bernie S. case would play out fully, starting with his sudden release in October 1995, his reimprisonment in January of 1996, and the efforts to get him released which lasted almost the entire year. This didn't end until after he was severely beaten in prison. Through this show, the whole world saw what happened and we believe that had an effect.

While this was the major story of this series of shows, there were many other topics. Among them: the continuing problems with ISDN as experienced firsthand by Emmanuel and Phiber, the Exon Bill, the wave of Kevin Mitnick books, the introduction of GSM, EZ Pass, and DVD's, web page hacking, plans for Beyond Hope and Hacking In Progress, and the introduction of "Off The Hook" to RealAudio.

Interviews with Scott Skinner, Bernie S., Dov Wisebrod, Jim Freund, Clint Danbury, Jon Littman, Jeff Goodell, Joker, Izzy, Master Chemist, Bernie S., Mike Yuhas, Pam from Esoteric, Rob Bernstein, Chris Neitzert, Alexis Rosen, Rop from Xs4all, Charles Platt, Manos the Technician, Roman Kazan, Lotek, and Mudge.
